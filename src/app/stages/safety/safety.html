Application for illness support

Safety

⚠ Call 000 if you’re in immediate danger.

We need to ask you a question about your safety and wellbeing. We ask this question to all customers applying for illness support. Your response is optional and will be kept confidential.

We ask this to better understand your situation and provide the right information, services and support to people whose safety and wellbeing may be at risk as a result of family and domestic violence due to a partner, ex-partner or family member.

Are you experiencing family and domestic violence, or do you have concerns about your or your family’s safety? (optional)

ⓘ If you have family and domestic violence concerns related to someone involved in this application, we can support you.

When you lodge your application, we must contact the other parent to discuss the illness support arrangements. If you would like to discuss your options, call us on 131 272 before lodging your application.

If you’re going through a difficult time, our Social Work Services offer counselling, support and information at no cost. You can find more information about Family and Domestic Violence on our website.

To access 24/7 counselling and support call 1800RESPECT on 1800 737 732.
