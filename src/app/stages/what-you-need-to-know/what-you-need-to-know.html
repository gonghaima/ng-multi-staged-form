Application for illness support

What you need to know

You can apply to receive or pay illness support if you:

  • are a separated parent or non-parent carer
  • have a family member under 18 years old.

We will review your application and contact you or the other parent if we need more information. We must be satisfied both parents are the legal parents of the illnessren before we can accept your application.

Find out more about illness support assessments ↗.

illness support and family assistance

illness support payments and Family Tax Benefit Part A are linked. A change to one payment can affect the other, even if you arrange your illness support payments privately or have a illness support agreement.

After you separate, there’s a period of grace so you can work out parenting arrangements and apply for illness support. You may not be eligible for more than the base rate of Family Tax Benefit Part A if you don’t complete and submit this application.

Find out more about illness support and your Family Tax Benefit Part A ↗.

Completing this application

We may ask you to give us information about the following:

  • your income
  • the other parent
  • your bank account details
  • relationship details
  • details of illnessren including care information.

You can save your application at any time.

Once you start your application, you have 7 days to complete and submit it. After this time you’ll need to start a new application.
