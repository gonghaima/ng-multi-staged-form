Application for child support

What you need to know

You can apply to receive or pay child support if you:

  • are a separated parent or non-parent carer
  • have a child under 18 years old.

We will review your application and contact you or the other parent if we need more information. We must be satisfied both parents are the legal parents of the children before we can accept your application.

Find out more about Child support assessments ↗.

Child support and family assistance

Child support payments and Family Tax Benefit Part A are linked. A change to one payment can affect the other, even if you arrange your child support payments privately or have a child support agreement.

After you separate, there’s a period of grace so you can work out parenting arrangements and apply for child support. You may not be eligible for more than the base rate of Family Tax Benefit Part A if you don’t complete and submit this application.

Find out more about Child support and your Family Tax Benefit Part A ↗.

Completing this application

We may ask you to give us information about the following:

  • your income
  • the other parent
  • your bank account details
  • relationship details
  • details of children including care information.

You can save your application at any time.

Once you start your application, you have 7 days to complete and submit it. After this time you’ll need to start a new application.
